Our Operational Framework

To provide world-class service, the Laguna Water District operates through a strategic Joint Venture Partnership. Here is how we work together for our consumers.

The Operator

LARC

Laguna Water District Aquatech Resources Corp.

  • Operations: Manages daily water supply, treatment, and distribution systems.
  • Customer Care: Handles all billing, collections, and customer service complaints.
  • Maintenance: Responsible for repairing leaks, meter management, and facility rehabilitation.
  • Investment: Finances the expansion and improvement of water infrastructure.
The Regulator

LWD

Laguna Water District

  • Policy & Planning: Sets service targets and long-term strategic obligations.
  • Asset Management: Retains ownership of facilities and conducts regular inspections.
  • Performance Review: Monitors LARC to ensure they meet quality and service standards.
  • Tariff Oversight: Manages and applies for water rate adjustments with government agencies.

Our History

The Laguna Water District (LWD) originated as the Los Baños Water District (LBWD), beginning as a municipal water provider. In 2016, it transformed under a joint venture (LARC) to improve operations across Los Baños, Bay, Calauan, and Victoria.

Despite early challenges regarding service reliability and infrastructure, a significant shift in management to Manila Water Philippine Ventures in 2024 has driven major improvements.

Today, these infrastructure investments have led to a wider scope of service and a rebranding into the Laguna Water District (LWD), committed to serving our communities better.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, adequate, safe, and potable water in Los Baños, Bay, Calauan, and Victoria.

Our Vision

To be the leading water service provider in the Southern Tagalog region.
