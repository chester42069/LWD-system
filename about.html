About Laguna Water District

Our History

The Laguna Water District (LWD) originated as the Los Baños Water District (LBWD), beginning as a municipal water provider. In 2016, it transformed under a joint venture (LARC) to improve operations across Los Baños, Bay, Calauan, and Victoria.

Despite early challenges regarding service reliability and infrastructure, a significant shift in management to Manila Water Philippine Ventures in 2024 has driven major improvements.

Today, these infrastructure investments have led to a wider scope of service and a rebranding into the Laguna Water District (LWD), committed to serving our communities better.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, adequate, safe, and potable water in Los Baños, Bay, Calauan, and Victoria.

Our Vision

To be the leading water service provider in the Southern Tagalog region.
