Our Services

Advanced Water Treatment

We are committed to delivering water that meets the highest quality standards. Our facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure safety and potability.

  • DMI-65 Filtration: Cutting-edge technology designed to remove iron, manganese, and arsenic.
  • PNSDW Compliance: Fully compliant with Philippine National Standards for Drinking Water.
  • Regular Testing: Continuous monitoring at pump stations like Umali, Bayog, and Vega.

Innovation

Pilot-tested success at Umali Pump Station in 2024.

Septic & Wastewater Management

Proper sanitation is key to public health. We provide comprehensive septic tank services to ensure wastewater is effectively managed and treated before it re-enters the ecosystem.

  • Desludging Services: Regular cleaning of septic tanks for households and businesses.
  • Treatment Process: Separation of solid waste and biological breakdown of organic matter.
  • Environmental Protection: Preventing contamination of groundwater and nearby water bodies.
